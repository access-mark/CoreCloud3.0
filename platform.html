From Fragmented Systems to a Single Intelligence Layer

Most organisations treat cloud, talent, hardware, and consulting as separate functions. Each produces data. None operate from a shared source of intelligence.

The CoreCloud AI Operations Engine unifies these signals into a single Adaptive Operations Graph, enabling decisions that reflect the full reality of your environment.

What the Adaptive Operations Graph Connects

Cloud Workloads & Cost Patterns

Usage, architecture, and billing data feed FinOps AI for optimisation and scenario modelling.

Talent & Delivery Performance

Role requirements, skills, culture markers, and outcomes shape the Talent Fit Engine.

Hardware Lifecycle & Demand

Purchase history, condition, and demand signals drive Marketplace Pricing AI.

Consulting Tasks & Risk

Engagement data, tasks, and risk profiles inform Consulting AI’s recommendations.

Partner Capability

Partner strengths, capacity, and track record are mapped for better allocation and collaboration.

Commercial Outcomes

Margin, success rates, and lifecycle value flow back into the graph to improve future decisions.

How the Engine Works

  1. Ingest: Securely connect to your existing cloud, talent, hardware, and operational data sources.
  2. Analyse: Identify patterns, anomalies, and correlations that matter to financial and delivery outcomes.
  3. Predict: Model cost curves, demand, risk, and fit over realistic time horizons.
  4. Recommend: Generate clear actions that people can execute or systems can automate.
  5. Improve: Learn from outcomes and partner data to continuously sharpen the model.
